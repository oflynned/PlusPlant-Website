OUR MISSION

Going Carbon Neutral Together

At PlusPlant, we are determined to overcome environemntal uncertainty by offsetting carbon emissions. We work with businesses and other organisations to calculate their unique carbon footprint. We then plant, manage, and monitor forestry tailored to individual business and organisations to ensure their carbon footprint is offset.


PlusPlant forests are based in Uganda just north of beautiful Lake Victoria. Together with local communities, we plant native tree species, which are both excellent at absorbing carbon and protecting local biodiversity.


So when you see a product with the PlusPlant logo, you'll know that it is a carbon neutral product, which is doing its small part ot make sure our planet is healthy and happy!
